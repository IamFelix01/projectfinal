ABOUT US

Save the truth as a memory, and help people to see !

Welcome to Photography Workshop, your number one source for all cameras. We are dedicated to giving you the best quality of pictures and photographic equipment, with a focus on precision, quality and price.
Founded in 2021 by our team of 4 people, Photography Workshop has come a long way since its inception in Settat-Morocco. When our team started, our passion to "save the truth as a memory, and help people to see!" drove us to build this store to meet your desires and demands, while satisfying you with the best types of cameras we have, so that our shop can offer you the world of photography. We are now serving customers all over the country, and we are excited to be able to turn our passion into our own website.
We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please do not hesitate to contact us.
